COVID19 Directives


Wear your face mask at all times. Make sure you have enough masks for your journey.

A mask should be replaced every 4 hours.

Bring your own hand sanitizer to disinfect surfaces regularly. Please pay close attention to the regulations for traveling with liquids in your carry-on luggage.

Maintain social distancing upon leaving the plane. Keep a physical distance of minimum 1.5 meters, and follow signage for your safety.

You are encouraged to travel as lightly as possible with all luggage checked-in except small hand luggage that fit under the seat.
